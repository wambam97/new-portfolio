Three Scripts

Type design Speculative design Hebrew Type Research based Editorial Award winning Book Design

Three Scripts is an award-winning typographic project exploring three new Hebrew writing systems. It investigates the "word" through three distinct lenses: as a magical force, a primordial concept, or a digital code. This study pushes the boundaries of how meaning and form coexist within the Hebrew language.

The project was honored with the AlefAlefAlef Design Award for 2025 graduates, recognizing excellence in Hebrew typography. It is also preserved in the Madaf.art archive, a digital project dedicated to the curation and preservation of local Israeli design.
