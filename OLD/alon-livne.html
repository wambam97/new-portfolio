Alon Livné: Spring 2027

Art Direction Editorial Design Book Design Brand Strategy AI Image Making
Commissioned for the Spring 2027 bridal collection, this editorial magazine serves as a comprehensive documentation of the design process. It bridges physical craftsmanship and editorial structure, translating intricate physical gowns into a cohesive print experience.
The layout pairs gown photography directly with detailed technical spec sheets. The project includes a full branding suite with a custom bag and linen perfume, ensuring the designer’s creative intent and consistent editorial structure are maintained through every touchpoint.
Generative AI was integrated to produce high-fidelity imagery. By achieving a seamless blend between digital generation and couture, the project demonstrates a capability to generate hyper-realistic assets alongside traditional photography.
The 126-page publication made its debut in April 2026 at New York Fashion Week. It served as a primary communication tool for international clients and industry leaders, acting as a visual showcase for the brand's global bridal couture line.
