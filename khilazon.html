Commissioned for the Spring 2027 bridal collection, this editorial magazine serves as a comprehensive documentation of the design process. It bridges physical craftsmanship and editorial structure, translating intricate physical gowns into a cohesive print experience.

The magazine made its professional debut in April 2026 at New York Fashion Week. It served as a primary touchpoint for international clients and industry leaders, acting as both a visual showcase and a vital communication tool for one of Israel's most prominent voices in global couture.
