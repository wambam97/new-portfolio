Flower

Illustration Character Design Storytelling Graphic Novel Social Commentary Dark Surrealism

Flower is an allegorical short comic that explores power dynamics, transactional morality, and survival in the city. The narrative centers on a confrontation between a restaurant server—who happens to be a bull—and a customer whose advances he rejects. In retaliation, the customer hatches a vengeful scheme alongside the restaurant owner, who willingly complies for a fee.

Drawing from personal experiences as a young man navigating urban spaces, the project serves as a dark visual commentary on the distortion of power, the vulnerability of youth, and the ease with which human capital is commodified by those with means.
